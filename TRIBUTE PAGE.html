Here we have the History of Steve Jobs :-

  • 1955:Steve Jobs was born in San Fransisco ,The son of Abdul Fattah Jandali and Joanne Schiebble.He is quickly adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.

  • 1960:The Family moved to the Suburban Town in Santaclara Country ,more famous under the name Silicon Valley.

  • 1976:Apple Computer Inc is incorporated by Steve Jobs ,Steve Wozniak and Ronwayne.

  • 1984:Apple's Macintosh was launched in a great fanfare at Apple's annual shareholder meeting.

  • 1985:Steve Jobs resigns from the Apple and started NeXT and Pixar with five other refugees from the Apple.

  • 1988:Pixar launched it's new Computer Graphics workstation ,the Pixar image Computer III.

  • 1989:Steve Jobs is named 'Enterpreneur of the Decade' by Inc.Magazine.

  • 1991:Steve Jobs marries Laurene Powell.

  • 1994:Pixar resumes Work on toystory.

  • 1998:Steve Jobs introduced Apple's Revolutionary iMAC.

  • 2000:Steve Jobs became Apple CEO.

  • 2007:Steve Jobs introduced Iphone Series.

  • 2011:Steve Jobs died due to Cancer at 56 in Palo Alto ,California.
